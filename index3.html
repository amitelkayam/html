Ramat Hanadiv and Zichron Yaacov

You must take road number 652 from Binyamina in the direction of Zichron Yaacov. Before Zichron Yaacov (about a kilometer and a half), turn left following the signs to "Gan Hanadiv" and continue until the parking lot. From the car park you can pass through the impressive gate where a symbol of the Rothschild family and we will immediately notice large and spectacular gardens carefully designed and examined around the tomb estate of the famous benefactor Baron Rothschild and his wife. The place has a variety of beautiful paths that stretch between plenty of vegetation, pools and waterfalls and spectacular views. And there is also the fragrance garden which is specially designed for the visually impaired and appeals in addition to the sense of sight to the sense of smell and the sense of touch.

Accessible road for the disabled on the passing route at spectacular points of interest: the entrance plaza, the fragrance garden, the palm garden, the Samaria Mountains lookout, the rose garden, the tomb plaza, the waterfall garden with a view of the Mediterranean Sea. The tomb cave has 5 steps to go down. On Saturday the cave is closed to visitors. Accessibility in the Generous Garden: Disabled parking. The road from the parking lot to the entrance is accessible. Accessible entrance. Stone-paved paths straight between the garden sites. Accessible Observations, Accessible Visitor Center. Disabled services with standard handholds and space for a wheelchair next to the toilet.

Ilanot Forest Botanical Garden

In the 1950s, trees from all over the world were planted in the forest soil to see if they would survive in the country. Then this forest located between the Moshava Kadima and Pardesiya was forgotten, but underwent a complete restoration and became a beautiful and completely accessible forest in the heart of the Sharon.

From the large parking lot there is easy access to the picnic tables, there are toilets and the walking paths are paved and accessible. The place also has a fun tree maze that is also adapted for the disabled. How to get there - Ilanot Forest is located on the eastern side of the "old" Tel Aviv-Haifa road (Road 4), between Dror Junction and Hasharon Junction (Beit Lid Junction). The site is open Sunday-Thursday during the day. Friday until 1 p.m. Important to know: The garden is not a place for a barbecue. You can also enjoy a great meal with a cooler.

The botanical garden is open to visitors as usual. The visitor center is open to the public in accordance with the guidelines of the Ministry of Health. To arrange a visit to the center, please contact Email: mmilanot@kkl.org.il
